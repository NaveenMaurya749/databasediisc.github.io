About

Welcome to Databased, the CSc Crew at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), where curiosity and innovation come together to create something extraordinary! We're a community of students passionate about diving deep into the world of computer science and related fields. We're here to provide a comprehensive resource for beginners to learn, grow, and create....[Read more]


As an excursion initialized by a team consisting of appreciably enthusiastic and passionate students from the UG club in Computer Science at IISc by the same name, Databased is the mother ship of collaborative exposure there is generated by the simple act of conjunction with multi-fold dimensionality of the creativity space and temporal space, following which is there infrastructure built by a team from this very community, that shall aid in further communicating discourse that shall be a benefactor to those who that constitute this community, to share the pool of knowledge that shall propagate further with time.
